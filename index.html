About

Welcome to Aydiv Crafts! We make exclusively handmade customized gifting solutions for all occasions! Our range is built around eco-friendly materials like paper, cardstock and wood, which do not harm the environment. At our boutique, we could do a completely custom-made gift and / or also create a replica of an item of your choice. We ensure that we deliver the right product with great detail.

Profile

Mrs. Vidya, the founder and owner of Aydiv Crafts, is an Engineering Graduate from BITS, Pilani and an ex-software engineer. Taking a break from her job, she and her husband together founded their technology startup, LadderMinds (www.ladderminds.com), at Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. Stepping into the world of entrepreneurship as the Co-Founder and CEO of LadderMinds, also paved the way to create Aydiv, a boutique that makes customized and personalized gifts for its customers.


She is also the Operations Director at Acodeidea Technologies Private Limited (www.acodeidea.com), another home startup providing consulting services to an overseas client with an employee count of ten people. Apart from managing the above startups, she also offers advisory services to the aeronautical startup Wethe5pilots (www.wethe5pilots.com), which is co-founded by her husband.
