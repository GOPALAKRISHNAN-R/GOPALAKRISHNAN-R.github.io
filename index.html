About Us

Welcome to ACTIVE DABBA. An exclusive online store for creative activity boxes involving themes like Crafts, Aeromodelling and so on. These kits are fabricated by a team of Subject and Field Experts and tested by various SME’s all around the world. Our kits are open ended and provide scope for imagination. We ensure that our kits would replace the user’s screen time into quality creative time.

They are based on S.T.E.M Learning principles and aim to improve the motor skills of the user apart from tapping their creative energy. These kits are tested and approved by age appropriate users across countries.

Helps in planning and organization
